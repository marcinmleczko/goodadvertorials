Exhibit C · The Method

Five reasons these "ugly" pages beat your pretty one.

Not theory. What's actually moving CVR in 2026. (Sources available on request. We have spreadsheets.)

  1. ✓ Reason №1

    Editorial presell. Not product page.

    Cold traffic can't handle a product page. It needs an editorial-feeling presell that lowers skepticism and raises belief before the offer. Every page here is a listicle or longform advertorial. Every. Single. One.

  2. ✓ Reason №2

    Message match, ad through CTA.

    The ad angle, the headline, the hook, the proof, the CTA — all the same thread. Congruence is the quiet 20% lift most brands leave on the table. (You are probably one of them.)

  3. ✓ Reason №3

    Proof density on every scroll.

    Stats, testimonials, before/after, press logos, FAQs, risk reversal. A reader should hit proof every three hundred pixels. Belief stacks. Like compound interest, but for skepticism.

  4. ✓ Reason №4

    One CTA. Repeated. Until the end.

    One offer. One button copy. Five to seven placements through the page. No "learn more" links bleeding intent to other routes. We do not believe in choice architecture. We believe in choice.

  5. ✓ Reason №5

    Shipped in days. Iterated weekly.

    An AI-assisted build stack on top of tested master templates. V1 live in three days, V2 after the first hundred thousand impressions. Your agency takes six weeks. We take three days. Math.
